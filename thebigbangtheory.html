The Big Bang Theory - A Little Bit of Sheldon

My uncle once said that I am like Sheldon Cooper from *The Big Bang Theory*. I have my own unique way of thinking, and sometimes, I can come off as a little quirky. One of his favorite quotes was: "Life is not the military. It's a volunteer organization." It's a reminder that not everything in life is as rigid or structured as the rules we follow at work or school.

Sheldon may be a genius, but he doesn't always understand the world in the same way others do. One of his most memorable lines is: "Any idiot can have a baby." It's funny, but it's also a reminder that raising a child is about more than just biology. Watch the scene here.
