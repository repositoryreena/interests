How to Win Friends and Influence People

Author: Dale Carnegie
Published: 1936
Genre: Self-Help, Personal Development
Synopsis:

*How to Win Friends and Influence People* is one of the most influential self-help books of all time. Written by Dale Carnegie, the book provides practical advice on how to improve your social skills, enhance your personal relationships, and become more persuasive. It is filled with timeless principles that focus on making others feel valued, building trust, and fostering goodwill.

The book is divided into several sections, each focusing on different aspects of social interactions, such as dealing with people, winning others over, and becoming a more effective communicator. It has been widely praised for its actionable advice and its ability to transform both personal and professional relationships.
