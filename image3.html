Romantic

Here are some iconic romantic movies with memorable quotes!

Romeo and Juliet

"Goodnight, goodnight! Parting is such sweet sorrow that I shall say goodnight till it be morrow."

This famous line from *Romeo and Juliet* is spoken by Juliet to Romeo. It expresses the bittersweet feeling of parting from a loved one, as she looks forward to reuniting with him the next day.

West Side Story

"When love comes so strong, there is no right or wrong, Your love, Is your life!"

This quote from *West Side Story* reflects the intense, passionate love between Tony and Maria. It suggests that love transcends logic or social conventions, becoming a force that defines their lives.

Moulin Rouge

"The greatest thing you'll ever learn is just to love and be loved in return."

This line from *Moulin Rouge!* encapsulates the theme of the movie, emphasizing the simple yet profound truth that the ability to love and be loved is the most powerful experience one can have in life.

The Princess Bride

"As you wish."

In *The Princess Bride*, this iconic line is spoken by Westley, showing his deep love and devotion to Buttercup. It signifies his willingness to do anything for her, expressing a love that is selfless and all-encompassing.

Moonstruck

"The stars are perfect. Not us. Not us! We are here to ruin ourselves and to break our hearts and love the wrong people and *die*."

This line from *Moonstruck* is a raw reflection on the imperfection of love. It acknowledges that love can be painful and complicated, often leading to heartbreak, but it is also an essential part of the human experience.

Pretty Woman

"She rescues him right back."

In *Pretty Woman*, this line highlights the reciprocal nature of the relationship between Vivian and Edward. While it may appear that Edward rescues Vivian, in truth, she brings her own form of healing and love to him as well.

27 Dresses

"You deserve to be the center of someone’s world, not just a part of someone’s special day."

This quote from *27 Dresses* speaks to the idea that everyone deserves to be cherished and valued as the most important person in someone's life, not just as a supporting role in someone else's happiness.
