The Great Gatsby

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Published: 1925

Genre: Novel, Classic American Literature

Synopsis:

*The Great Gatsby* is a novel set in the Roaring Twenties that tells the story of Jay Gatsby, a mysterious and wealthy man who spends his days throwing lavish parties in hopes of rekindling a past romance with Daisy Buchanan. Narrated by Nick Carraway, who moves next door to Gatsby, the novel explores themes of love, obsession, social class, and the American Dream. It is a poignant critique of the hollowness and disillusionment that can lie behind wealth and success.
