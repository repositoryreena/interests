1984

Author: George Orwell

Published: 1949

Genre: Dystopian Fiction, Political Fiction

Synopsis:

*1984* is a dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society governed by "The Party" and led by the omnipresent figure known as Big Brother. The story follows Winston Smith, a low-ranking member of the Party, who secretly rebels against the oppressive regime and its surveillance state. The novel explores themes of censorship, government surveillance, mind control, and the erosion of individual freedoms, making it a powerful critique of totalitarianism and a timeless warning about the dangers of state power.
