Diary of a Wimpy Kid

*Diary of a Wimpy Kid* (2010) is a comedy film based on the book series by Jeff Kinney. It follows Greg Heffley, a middle schooler who struggles with the trials of growing up, school life, and family dynamics. However, the character I relate to most is Greg's older brother, Rodrick.

Rodrick is rebellious, lazy, and unapologetically indifferent to school, often showing a disdain for authority. One of his most memorable quotes is, "What can I learn in school that I can't teach myself?" This attitude resonates with me because, like Rodrick, I often question the conventional paths and prefer to find my own way. His character embodies that carefree spirit of not taking life too seriously, and I admire how he follows his own rules, regardless of what others think.
