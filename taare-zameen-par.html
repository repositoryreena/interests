Taare Zameen Par

"Taare Zameen Par" (Stars on Earth) is a 2007 Bollywood drama film directed by Aamir Khan that deals with the pressures children face in education and the impact of a strict upbringing. It highlights the journey of a young boy struggling with dyslexia and the importance of understanding and support from family and teachers.
