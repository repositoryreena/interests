Embracing Laziness

Take a break and explore some iconic movies that capture the essence of being lazy. But for me, laziness isn't just about taking it easy—it's about being nonchalant. I don't care about much, and that makes me different from others who are constantly concerned with every little thing. They care about what others think, they care about all the drama, but I don’t. Whether I live or die, it doesn’t matter to me. People tell me I shouldn't be angry, but then they get furious about the smallest things. Take the Oscars, for example—when they read the wrong name, you’d think the world was ending. Meanwhile, Ryan Gosling just found it all hilarious. My nonchalance lets me see things from a whole different angle, and honestly, it makes me feel like I’m above all the nonsense that others get so worked up about.
