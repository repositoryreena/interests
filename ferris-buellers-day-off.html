Ferris Bueller's Day Off

Ferris Bueller's Day Off (1986) is a classic teen comedy directed by John Hughes. The film follows Ferris, a high school senior who decides to skip school for a day of fun in Chicago with his best friend Cameron and his girlfriend Sloane. Ferris' antics are legendary as he outsmarts his principal and teachers, proving that sometimes it's okay to just take a break and do absolutely nothing.
