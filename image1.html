The Perks of Being a Wallflower

Author: Stephen Chbosky
Published: 1999
Genre: Young Adult, Coming-of-age
Synopsis:

*The Perks of Being a Wallflower* follows Charlie, a shy and introspective high school freshman, as he navigates the trials and tribulations of adolescence. Through a series of letters, Charlie shares his thoughts on friendship, love, and the challenges of growing up. As Charlie begins to make friends with step-siblings Patrick and Sam, he discovers the complex beauty of life and the darker parts of his own past.
