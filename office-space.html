Office Space

*Office Space* (1999) is a satirical comedy film directed by Mike Judge, highlighting the absurdities of corporate culture. The film follows Peter Gibbons, a software engineer stuck in a mundane office job at Initech, as he seeks a way out of the soul-sucking routine.

I personally relate to Milton Waddams, the character who often talks a lot without being listened to. Like Milton, I tend to ramble on and on, often to no avail. His quiet frustration with his office environment mirrors my own experience of being overlooked, but what stands out is how Milton’s persistence and constant talking keep him memorable — even when no one else is paying attention. It’s a reminder that sometimes, even the most unnoticed voices have something important to say.
