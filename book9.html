The Outsiders

Author: S.E. Hinton

Published: 1967

Genre: Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Synopsis:

*The Outsiders* tells the story of Ponyboy Curtis, a 14-year-old boy who belongs to the "Greasers," a working-class gang in a small American town. The novel explores the struggles between the Greasers and the more affluent "Socs" (Socials). As tensions rise between the two groups, Ponyboy navigates his own identity, relationships with family and friends, and the challenges of growing up. The novel touches on themes of class, loyalty, and the quest for personal belonging, making it a staple of young adult literature.
