The Little Mermaid: Ariel's Transformation

Ariel, the curious and innocent mermaid, makes a deal with the sea witch Ursula to gain a human form and pursue her love for Prince Eric. While Ariel transforms into a human, it's worth noting that Ursula, in her human disguise, is arguably more beautiful than Ariel. Ursula's human form, with her allure and confidence, contrasts greatly with Ariel's more naive, youthful appearance. This transformation leads to a dramatic shift in the story, highlighting the dangers of dealing with dark forces.
