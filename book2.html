On the Road

Author: Jack Kerouac

Published: 1957

Genre: Beat Generation, Road Novel

Synopsis:

*On the Road* is a semi-autobiographical novel by Jack Kerouac that chronicles the author's cross-country travels with his friends. The book captures the restless spirit and search for meaning that defines the Beat Generation. It explores themes of freedom, rebellion, self-discovery, and the quest for spiritual truth as the characters engage in a series of spontaneous and often reckless adventures.
