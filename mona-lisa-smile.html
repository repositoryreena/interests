Mona Lisa Smile: Challenging Gender Roles

*Mona Lisa Smile* (2003) explores the challenges faced by women in the 1950s as they navigate societal expectations about marriage, career, and education. The film follows Katherine Watson, a progressive art history professor at Wellesley College, as she encourages her female students to think critically about their roles in a patriarchal society.
