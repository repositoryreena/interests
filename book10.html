Pride and Prejudice

Author: Jane Austen

Published: 1813

Genre: Romance, Social Criticism

Synopsis:

*Pride and Prejudice* follows the headstrong Elizabeth Bennet as she navigates issues of class, love, and marriage in early 19th-century England. When Elizabeth meets the aloof and seemingly arrogant Mr. Darcy, she forms a quick negative opinion of him. However, as the story unfolds, Elizabeth's feelings toward Darcy shift as she learns more about his true character, and Darcy also begins to admire her intelligence and independence. Through sharp social commentary, the novel explores themes of pride, prejudice, and personal growth.
