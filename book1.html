The Fountainhead

Author: Ayn Rand

Published: 1943

Genre: Philosophical Fiction

Synopsis:

*The Fountainhead* follows the story of Howard Roark, an individualistic and innovative architect who refuses to conform to traditional designs and expectations. His uncompromising attitude towards art and life leads him to battle against societal norms and the established powers in the architectural world. The novel is a passionate exploration of individualism, integrity, and the pursuit of personal vision.
