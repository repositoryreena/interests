Clueless

Director: Amy Heckerling
Release Year: 1995
Genre: Comedy, Romance, Coming-of-age
Synopsis:

*Clueless* is a 1995 teen comedy film written and directed by Amy Heckerling. The movie is a modern adaptation of Jane Austen's classic novel *Emma*, set in a Beverly Hills high school. It follows the life of Cher Horowitz, a wealthy and popular teenager who thrives on matchmaking her friends and trying to improve her social standing. Along the way, Cher learns important lessons about love, relationships, and the true meaning of happiness.

The film is a beloved cult classic, known for its witty dialogue, iconic fashion, and the charismatic performance of Alicia Silverstone as Cher. It has become a defining movie of 1990s teen culture and continues to inspire fans with its timeless humor and memorable quotes.
