Naked Lunch

Author: William S. Burroughs

Published: 1959

Genre: Surrealist, Beat Literature, Science Fiction

Synopsis:

*Naked Lunch* is a groundbreaking and controversial novel by William S. Burroughs, known for its fragmented narrative and its exploration of drug addiction, control, and societal decay. The novel follows the protagonist, William Lee, through a nightmarish and surreal series of events, including encounters with drug dealers, government agents, and various bizarre characters. *Naked Lunch* challenges conventional storytelling and is considered one of the most important works of the Beat Generation, pushing the boundaries of literary form and content.
