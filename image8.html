While I appreciate the talents of Elizabeth Banks, I can't bring myself to enjoy the *Barbie* movie or *Mrs. America*. They are brilliantly crafted and thought-provoking, but I find them ultimately sad. The *Barbie* movie, for instance, presents a world where the Barbies represent men in reality and the Kens represent women. It feels more like a documentary than a piece of entertainment, and honestly, I'm not a fan of documentaries. They leave me reflecting on things that are hard to accept, and it takes away from my enjoyment of film.
