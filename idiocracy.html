Idiocracy: A Glimpse into the Future

*Idiocracy* is a satirical science fiction comedy that imagines a future where society has become so dumbed down that basic intelligence is almost nonexistent. The film follows an average man who is unintentionally frozen for 500 years and wakes up to find that the world has become incredibly stupid. It's a hilarious yet terrifying look at how society could potentially evolve.

The movie asks important questions about the future of human intelligence and the consequences of anti-intellectualism. It highlights the importance of critical thinking and education in a world that increasingly values entertainment and superficiality over knowledge.
